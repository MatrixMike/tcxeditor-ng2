Ever left your Garmin running at the end of a ride? All your hard earned averages declined pointlessly?

This online editor enables you simply and easily to delete points from a tcx file. It then recalculates aggregate ride data, and returns a replacement file to use with your favourite analytics tools.

Give it a try or see it in action with some existing data. Note that there will be a short pause after you upload data, and that the site is currently not optimised for small devices.
